Text-Based Game

This is a text-based game I made made using the program Twine (http://twinery.org/)

It started as a school project, but I've extended it. This is the first draft of the complete game. I'm still editing and testing it. In its current state, a play through can take about 30 minutes.

A playable demo is included below. A scrollbar will appear and can be utilized if a passage is longer than the window. You may also click 'Play Now' to open the game in a new tab.
